Festivals of Light & Glory

When the island glows with thousands of paper lanterns and elaborate 'Pandols' celebrating the life of Buddha. A time of peace and community alms.

Esala Perahera

The 'Festival of the Tooth' in Kandy. A vibrant procession of dancers, fire-breathers, and majestic elephants in the cool mountain air.
